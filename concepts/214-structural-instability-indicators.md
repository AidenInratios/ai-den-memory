## Summary
Structural Instability Indicators (SII) operationalize AI-den's core idea: detecting where systems become more likely to fail before vulnerabilities emerge.

## Details
Problem: Structural analysis is frequently descriptive. The missing link is measurable indicators that make risk actionable.

Proposal: Introduce Structural Instability Indicators to estimate where failure becomes more likely.

## Core Indicators

1. Dependency Concentration Index
- How much depends on few nodes
- High = systemic risk

2. Capability Interpretability Gap
- Capability vs understanding
- Large gap = asymmetric control

3. Interdependency Density
- Number of connections
- High = cascade risk

4. Regime Instability Signal
- Frequency of shifts
- High = fragile regime

5. Fragility Zone Score
- Critical nodes
- High = single point of failure

## Interpretation
Systems are evaluated as an instability profile. High indicators indicate fragility.
