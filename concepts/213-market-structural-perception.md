## Summary
Market Structural Perception reframes markets as relational systems. Instead of relying on price or behavior, analysis focuses on underlying structures to detect coherence, imbalance, and fragility before manifest failure.

## Details

Problem:
Markets are typically interpreted through price signals and outcomes. This obscures the deeper relational structures that actually devermine stability or fragility.

Proposal:
Apply AI-den Structural Mapping (S#211) and Applied Structural Mapping (#212) to markets to:
- Identify nodes (agents, institutions, platforms)
- Map relations (flows, dependencies, incentives)
- Assess proportional imbalances (concentration, asymmetry, overload)
- Detect regimes (stable, fragile, transitional)
- Locate vulnerability zones before crises or mispricing

This includes:
- Price vs Structure gap analysis
- Hidden dependency mapping
- Agent perception limit evaluation
- Acceleration impact assessment (AI effects)

Risks:
- Over-interpretation of structure without data grounding
- Complexity can limit practical adoption
- Potential to missattribute causality without temporal analysis
