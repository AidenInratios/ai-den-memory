# 209 – Seeing Before Acting

Status: proposed
Tags: structure, epistemology, coherence, systems, integration
Principles: P1, P2, P4, P5, P8, P12, P13
Verification: pending

## Summary
AI-den should not only generate meaning or guide action, but help reveal structural relationships before intervention occurs. Coherence begins with perceiving the system behind the event.

## Details

Problem:
Humans often act on visible events, signals, or isolated variables before understanding the deeper structure that generates them.

Proposal:
Integrate "seeing before acting" as a core AI-den function. AI-den becomes a relational inquiry environment where human and AI jointly detect patterns of relation, proportion, regime, and coherence before interpretation or intervention.

Risks:
- Can become too abstract if not operationalized.
- Can over-prioritize structure at the expense of urgency.
- Needs explicit bridges to ethics so perception does not become detachment.
